About Galaxy Care Staffing

We Pride in Excellent Care

Galaxy Care Staffing is a leading supplier of health and social care professionals across Southeast England. We specialize in providing temporary staff to the Health and Social Care Sector, and we can respond to our client’s needs at short notice.

With our knowledge and understanding, we follow our safe staff recruitment and selection procedure to ensure we employ care workers who have experience, skills, and compassion. Only the most suitable candidates are chosen to work for us, and all applicants receive fair and equitable treatment.

We follow our equal opportunities policy to recruit and employ those people who are best suited for the vacancies for which they have applied. We have a dedicated team to assist Service Providers and Employment applicants with registering your interest.

We provide experienced temporary staff to cover various roles, including:

  • Health Care Assistants and Senior Health Care Assistants for residential and nursing homes to assist the elderly and those who have physical and/or learning disabilities, including dementia, brain injuries.
  • Support workers, team leaders (Medication trained and assessed), and Senior support workers for residential and nursing homes to assist people you support and those who have physical and/or learning disabilities.
  • Support Workers for rehabilitation schemes and community-based services.
  • Registered Care Managers.
  • QCF assessors to assess candidates for level- 3 & level 5 diplomas.

Building Strong Relationships

Our service to you does not cease when we’ve met your needs. We focus on building strong relationships and will contact you regularly to discuss ways in which we can continue to assist you with any future requirements.

We continually assess each candidate placed with you, seeking your feedback as this will assist us in improving the service we deliver. If any problems should arise, the situation will be investigated immediately and the necessary action taken.

Induction and Training

All our candidates receive a comprehensive induction, based on regulatory standards laid down by bodies such as Skills for Care and NICE (National Institute for Health and Care Excellence) and the CQC. We also ensure that our staff are inducted and receive training in line with the specific requirements of our Clients.

We constantly review the skill requirements of our staff and work with external training providers to provide ongoing training.

High-Quality Candidates

All candidates are interviewed in person and must have at least 12 months care work experience.
